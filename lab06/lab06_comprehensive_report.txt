OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This lab explores Hopfield networks as energy-based models for:
  1. Associative memory and error correction (Problem 1)
  2. Constraint satisfaction problems (Problem 2: Eight-Rook)
  3. Combinatorial optimization (Problem 3: TSP)

Hopfield networks use a Lyapunov energy function that decreases
monotonically under asynchronous updates, ensuring convergence to
stable fixed points. By carefully designing the energy landscape,
we can encode both memory patterns and constraint optimization problems.
